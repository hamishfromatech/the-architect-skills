# Email Composer

Compose professional business emails with proper structure, tone, and formatting.

## Overview

The Email Composer skill helps you create well-structured business emails with appropriate tone, formatting, and content. It supports formal, semi-formal, and casual email styles.

## Quick Start

### Trigger Phrases

Use these phrases to activate the skill:
- "write an email"
- "compose an email"
- "draft an email"
- "send an email"
- "create an email template"
- "write a professional email"
- "email response"

## Capabilities

- Compose formal, semi-formal, and casual business emails
- Generate email templates for common scenarios
- Format emails with proper greetings, body structure, and sign-offs
- Adapt tone based on recipient relationship and context

## Email Types

| Type | Purpose | Key Elements |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Introduction | Establish new contact | Context, purpose, call to action |
| Request | Ask for information/action | Clear request, deadline, reason |
| Follow-up | Check status/progress | Reference, status check, next steps |
| Proposal | Present ideas/offers | Value proposition, details, CTA |
| Apology | Address mistakes | Acknowledge, apologize, solution |
| Thank You | Express gratitude | Specific thanks, impact, forward look |

## Tone Guidelines

| Tone | When to Use | Salutation | Sign-off |
|------|-------------|------------|----------|
| Formal | Executives, clients, first contact | Dear [Title] [Last Name] | Sincerely |
| Semi-Formal | Regular clients, colleagues | Dear [First Name] | Best regards |
| Casual | Team members, close colleagues | Hi [First Name] | Thanks/Best |
