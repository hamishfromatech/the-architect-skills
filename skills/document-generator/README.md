# Document Generator

Generate professional business documents including proposals, contracts, reports, memos, and letters.

## Overview

The Document Generator skill creates structured business documents from templates with proper formatting and content organization.

## Quick Start

### Trigger Phrases

Use these phrases to activate the skill:
- "create a document"
- "generate a document"
- "write a proposal"
- "create a contract"
- "draft an agreement"
- "make a report"
- "generate a letter"
- "create a memo"

## Capabilities

- Generate proposals, contracts, reports, memos, and letters
- Create agreements, invoices, and other business documents
- Apply consistent formatting and structure
- Support multiple output formats (Markdown, HTML)

## Supported Document Types

| Document Type | Description | Key Sections |
|--------------|-------------|--------------|
| Proposal | Business proposals for clients/partners | Executive summary, scope, timeline, pricing |
| Contract | Legal agreements | Parties, terms, conditions, signatures |
| Report | Business reports | Summary, findings, recommendations |
| Memo | Internal communications | Purpose, details, action items |
| Letter | Formal correspondence | Salutation, body, sign-off |
| Agreement | Non-binding agreements | Terms, responsibilities, duration |
| Invoice | Billing documents | Line items, amounts, payment terms |

## Document Structure

### Proposals
```
1. Title Page
2. Executive Summary
3. Problem Statement / Background
4. Proposed Solution
5. Scope of Work
6. Timeline
7. Pricing / Investment
8. Terms & Conditions
```

### Contracts
```
1. Parties Identification
2. Recitals / Background
3. Terms and Conditions
4. Obligations
5. Payment Terms
6. Duration and Termination
7. Signatures
```
