# Business Document Examples

This document provides example business documents for various scenarios.

## Proposal Example

```markdown
# Website Redesign Proposal

**Prepared for:** Acme Corporation
**Prepared by:** TechCorp Solutions
**Date:** November 15, 2024
**Version:** 1.0

---

## Executive Summary

TechCorp Solutions proposes a comprehensive website redesign for Acme Corporation
that will modernize your digital presence, improve user engagement, and drive
conversion growth. Our approach combines cutting-edge design with proven UX
principles to deliver measurable results.

---

## Problem Statement

Acme Corporation's current website presents several challenges:

1. **Outdated Design**: The current design does not reflect Acme's brand positioning
2. **Poor Mobile Experience**: 67% of users access via mobile, yet the site is not responsive
3. **Low Conversion Rate**: Current conversion rate is 1.2%, below industry average of 2.5%
4. **SEO Limitations**: Poor technical foundation affects search visibility

---

## Proposed Solution

### Phase 1: Discovery & Strategy (Weeks 1-2)
- Stakeholder interviews
- User research and persona development
- Competitive analysis
- Technical audit

### Phase 2: Design (Weeks 3-6)
- Information architecture
- Wireframing
- Visual design concepts
- Prototyping and testing

### Phase 3: Development (Weeks 7-12)
- Front-end development
- CMS implementation
- Third-party integrations
- Quality assurance

### Phase 4: Launch & Optimization (Weeks 13-14)
- Content migration
- Go-live support
- Performance optimization
- Training and documentation

---

## Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Key Milestones |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| Discovery | 2 weeks | Strategy document approved |
| Design | 4 weeks | Final designs approved |
| Development | 6 weeks | Site ready for testing |
| Launch | 2 weeks | Site live |

**Total Duration:** 14 weeks

---

## Investment

| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Discovery & Strategy | $15,000 |
| Design | $25,000 |
| Development | $40,000 |
| Launch & Training | $10,000 |
| **Total Project** | **$90,000** |

**Payment Terms:**
- 30% upon project start
- 40% upon design approval
- 30% upon project completion

---

## Terms & Conditions

1. Project scope as defined in this proposal
2. Additional revisions beyond 2 rounds billed at $150/hour
3. Hosting and maintenance not included
4. 90-day warranty on all deliverables
5. Intellectual property transfers upon final payment

---

**Acceptance:**

_____________________________
Client Signature / Date

_____________________________
TechCorp Solutions / Date
```

## Memo Example

```markdown
# MEMORANDUM

**TO:** All Department Heads
**FROM:** Human Resources Department
**DATE:** November 15, 2024
**SUBJECT:** Remote Work Policy Update - Effective December 1, 2024

---

## Purpose

This memo outlines changes to the company's remote work policy, effective
December 1, 2024.

---

## Background

Following the successful pilot program and employee feedback surveys, the
company has decided to permanently implement a hybrid work model.

---

## Policy Changes

### Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period are
eligible for hybrid work arrangements.

### Schedule
- Maximum 3 remote work days per week
- Minimum 2 in-office days per week
- Core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (flexible within those hours)

### Approval Process
1. Employee submits request through HR portal
2. Manager approval required
3. HR reviews for eligibility
4. Final approval within 5 business days

### Requirements
- Dedicated workspace
- Reliable internet connection
- Availability during core hours
- Attendance at all scheduled meetings

---

## Action Required

Please communicate these changes to your teams by November 25, 2024.

---

## Questions

Contact HR at hr@company.com or extension 1234.
```
