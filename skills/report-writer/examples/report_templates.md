# Report Templates

This document provides templates for various business reports.

## Status Report Template

```markdown
# Status Report: [Project/Task Name]

**Period:** [Start Date] - [End Date]
**Author:** [Name]
**Status:** 🟢 On Track / 🟡 At Risk / 🔴 Off Track

---

## Summary
[2-3 sentence overview of current status]

---

## Completed This Period
- ✅ [Accomplishment 1]
- ✅ [Accomplishment 2]
- ✅ [Accomplishment 3]

---

## In Progress
- 🔄 [Task 1] - [Progress %] - ETA: [Date]
- 🔄 [Task 2] - [Progress %] - ETA: [Date]

---

## Upcoming
- 📋 [Task 1] - Start: [Date]
- 📋 [Task 2] - Start: [Date]

---

## Blockers & Risks
| Issue | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|-------|--------|------------|-------|
| [Issue] | [Impact level] | [Plan] | [Name] |

---

## Metrics
| Metric | Target | Actual | Trend |
|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| [Metric 1] | [Target] | [Actual] | 📈/📉/➡️ |
| [Metric 2] | [Target] | [Actual] | 📈/📉/➡️ |

---

## Next Steps
1. [Next step 1]
2. [Next step 2]
3. [Next step 3]

---

## Next Period Goals
- [Goal 1]
- [Goal 2]
- [Goal 3]

---

*Report generated on [Date]*
```
