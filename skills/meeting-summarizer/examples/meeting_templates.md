# Meeting Notes Templates

This document provides templates for various types of meeting notes.

## Standard Meeting Notes Template

```markdown
# Meeting Notes: [Meeting Title]

**Date:** [Date]
**Time:** [Start Time] - [End Time]
**Location:** [Physical location or virtual platform]
**Organizer:** [Name]
**Note Taker:** [Name]

---

## Attendees
- [Name 1] - [Role/Department]
- [Name 2] - [Role/Department]
- [Name 3] - [Role/Department]

## Apologies
- [Name] - [Reason for absence]

---

## Agenda Items

### 1. [Agenda Topic 1]
- Discussion points
- Key insights
- Outcome/Decision

### 2. [Agenda Topic 2]
- Discussion points
- Key insights
- Outcome/Decision

---

## Key Decisions
| Decision | Made By | Date |
|----------|---------|------|
| [Decision 1] | [Name] | [Date] |
| [Decision 2] | [Name] | [Date] |

---

## Action Items
| Task | Owner | Due Date | Status |
|------|-------|----------|--------|
| [Task 1] | [Name] | [Date] | Pending |
| [Task 2] | [Name] | [Date] | Pending |

---

## Risks & Blockers
- [Risk/Blocker 1]
- [Risk/Blocker 2]

---

## Next Steps
1. [Next step 1]
2. [Next step 2]

---

## Next Meeting
**Date:** [Date]
**Time:** [Time]
**Agenda:** [Brief preview of topics]

---

*Notes prepared by [Name] on [Date]*
```

## Standup Meeting Template

```markdown
# Daily Standup: [Team Name]

**Date:** [Date]
**Time:** [Time]

## Updates

### [Name 1]
**Completed:** [What was done yesterday]
**In Progress:** [What's being worked on today]
**Blockers:** [Any blockers or "None"]

### [Name 2]
**Completed:** [What was done yesterday]
**In Progress:** [What's being worked on today]
**Blockers:** [Any blockers or "None"]

### [Name 3]
**Completed:** [What was done yesterday]
**In Progress:** [What's being worked on today]
**Blockers:** [Any blockers or "None"]

---

## Action Items
- [Action item with owner]

## Parking Lot (for after standup)
- [Topic to discuss separately]
```

## Decision Meeting Template

```markdown
# Decision Meeting: [Topic]

**Date:** [Date]
**Attendees:** [Names]

## Background
[Context and why this decision is needed]

## Options Considered

### Option 1: [Name]
- **Pros:** [Advantages]
- **Cons:** [Disadvantages]
- **Cost:** [Estimated cost]
- **Timeline:** [Timeline]

### Option 2: [Name]
- **Pros:** [Advantages]
- **Cons:** [Disadvantages]
- **Cost:** [Estimated cost]
- **Timeline:** [Timeline]

### Option 3: [Name]
- **Pros:** [Advantages]
- **Cons:** [Disadvantages]
- **Cost:** [Estimated cost]
- **Timeline:** [Timeline]

---

## Discussion Summary
[Key points from the discussion]

---

## Decision

**Decision:** [Final decision]
**Rationale:** [Why this option was chosen]
**Made By:** [Name/Role]
**Date:** [Date]

---

## Action Items
- [ ] [Action 1] - @[Owner] - Due: [Date]
- [ ] [Action 2] - @[Owner] - Due: [Date]

---

## Review Date
**Next Review:** [Date] - [Reason for review]
```

## One-on-One Meeting Template

```markdown

**Date:** [Date]
**Time:** [Time]

---

## Check-in
[How are things going? Any personal updates?]

---

## Progress Update
- **Accomplishments:** [What went well]
- **Challenges:** [What's been difficult]

---

## Discussion Topics
1. [Topic 1]
2. [Topic 2]
3. [Topic 3]

---

## Feedback
### From Manager:
[Feedback points]

### From Employee:
[Feedback points]

---

## Goals & Development
- **Short-term goal:** [Goal]
- **Long-term goal:** [Goal]
- **Development opportunity:** [Training, project, etc.]

---

## Action Items
- [ ] [Action 1] - @[Owner]
- [ ] [Action 2] - @[Owner]

---

## Next Meeting
**Date:** [Date]
**Topics to discuss:** [Preview]
```
